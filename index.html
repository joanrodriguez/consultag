Dr. Michael Mayer

Data scientist

Michael joined Consult AG in 2010 as a data scientist. Equipped with a PhD in mathematical statistics, he is responsible for all kind of statistical tasks including regression, classification, cluster analysis, survival analysis and sample size calculations. He occasionally helps others on questions and aswers website Cross Validated. His favorite statistical programming package is R.

Before his time at Consult AG, Michael worked as pricing actuarian for the Allianz Group and as biostatistician at the Swiss Group for Clinical Cancer Research.

Besides that, Michael is lecturer for continuing studies in statistics at the University of Bern.
